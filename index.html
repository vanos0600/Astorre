Astorre in role, retail under control

From delivery to shelf — fully aligned by AI

Astorre unites predictive staffing, AI-driven task orchestration, and piece-rate pay in a single platform — giving UK retailers leaner costs, fuller shelves, and engaged teams.

From chaos to clarity

The Problem

  • 50% staff churn in convenience retail.
  • Seasonal peaks lift labour demand 40%.
  • Stockouts cost over £1bn a year.
  • Systems don't connect: staff, stock, pay misaligned.

The Solution

  • One loop: from delivery to shelf.
  • Predictive staffing: AI forecasts demand.
  • AI tasking: roles and shifts set on the fly.
  • Piece-rate pay: fair, legal, motivating.
  • One dashboard: managers and staff see all.
